CONDITIONAL BA:if ‘the first thing’, then ‘the second thing’. This ba form can mean ‘if’ and ‘when’. It implies a very strong connection between the two events. The statme following the ば form is generally used with assumptions or general facts, but CANNOT BE USED TO EXPRESS WILL, INTENTION OR COMMAND. It needs to be a hypothetical situation. So it’s often not used with your own action because you can decide what your outcome is
